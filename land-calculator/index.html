Standard Bigha (33 Decimal): This is the official government standard across Bangladesh. It is universally used for all legal deeds, government land registries, and taxation. (1 Bigha = 33 Decimal = 14,400 Sq Ft).

Regional Bigha (52 Decimal): Used locally in specific rural regions of Bangladesh (e.g., parts of Rajshahi/Northern areas) based on older customary measurements set by historical local Zamindars. Always clarify which "Bigha" is meant during local transactions!
