User Guide & Features

How to Create a QR Code for Free

  1. Select your content type: Choose from URL, Text, Email, Phone, WhatsApp, SMS, vCard, Location, or WiFi using the top navigation tabs.
  2. Enter your details: Fill in the required fields. For example, enter your website URL or your WiFi network credentials.
  3. Customize the design (Optional): Open the accordion menus to change the foreground and background colors, adjust the padding, and even upload your own custom logo.
  4. Adjust the shape: Stand out by changing the standard square shapes to dots, rounded corners, or classy patterns.
  5. Download the QR Code: Use the quality slider to pick your desired resolution, then click the "Download PNG" or ".SVG" button to save your generated QR code locally.
